Key Cities

Weekly FORECAST

Today until Tomorrow (December 05), cloudy skies with light rains will prevail over Batanes, Apayao, Cagayan including the Calayan and Babuyan group of islands, and Isabela due to the Amihan (Northeast Monsoon). Meanwhile, Metro Manila and the rest of Luzon will have partly cloudy to cloudy skies with isolated light rains. Visayas and Mindanao will have generally fair weather conditions apart from isolated rainshowers or thunderstorms.
By Sunday until Tuesday (December 6 - 8), Batanes, Calayan and Babuyan group of islands will experience cloudy skies with light rains. Metro Manila and the rest of the country will have partly cloudy to cloudy skies with isolated rainshowers or thunderstorms
Throughout the rest of the outlook period (December 9 - 11), the whole country will have generally fair weather conditions apart from isolated rainshowers or thunderstorms.
No Tropical Cyclone is expected to enter or develop inside the Philippine Area of Responsibility (PAR). However, this outlook will be updated as soon as significant changes in the weather pattern occur.
